Kevin Suttle

Designer. Developer. Writer. Presenter.

With strong roots in user interface, interaction, and experience design, Kevin approaches usability from a holistic, ‘big-picture’ perspective. Ranging anywhere from information architecture, to performance optimization, to hand-crafting UIs for mobile and TV, Kevin's deep fascination with the way people communicate through technology is what drives him to make things “the way they should be.”

An accomplished writer, Kevin served as a Technical Reviewer and author for a handful of O’Reilly Media publications and is currently a contributor for UX Magazine.

Kevin is an Adobe Community Professional, and speaks at a number of the top conferences in the industry including FITC and OSCON. He’s earned numerous accolades, including being listed among the “Top Presentations of the Day” and having the “Most discussed presentation on Twitter” by Slideshare.
